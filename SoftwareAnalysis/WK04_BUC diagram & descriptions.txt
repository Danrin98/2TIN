-> UML - Unified Modeling Language
	- Graphical representations (= diagrams) of certain aspects of the information system
		=> Goal : support for business modeling 
		=> Introduced in RUP
		=> Business actor : external person or system interacting with business
		=> Business use case : should produce result of observable value to business actor
			- BOTH are not defined in UML standard

-> Business use case models 

	- For simple business situations
	- BUC : a way in which a customer or some other party can make use of the business to get the result they want (encompasses all the necessary activities)
	- SUC : a way in which a customer or some other party can make use of the system to get the result they want

	=> See BUC diagrams.drawio
